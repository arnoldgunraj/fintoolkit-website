Does it work with my bank?
BankBridge supports 14+ named bank formats and auto-detects any CSV with recognizable column headers. Start a free trial and test it with your actual bank exports before purchasing.

It complements them. Native bank feeds are great when they work, but they require IT setup, cost extra, and don't cover every bank. BankBridge handles the banks that fall through the gaps — especially during M&A transitions when you're working across multiple ERP instances.
